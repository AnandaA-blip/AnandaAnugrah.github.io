About Me

Where Clinical Knowledge Meets Data Thinking

I'm a fresh graduate in Medical Laboratory Technology with a deep interest in the data side of healthcare. During my studies, I noticed how much decision-making in clinical environments depended on data — and how often that data was underutilized. That curiosity pushed me to learn SQL, Python, Tableau, and Looker Studio.

My background gives me something most data analysts don't have: direct understanding of clinical workflows, lab processes, and healthcare KPIs. I don't just analyze healthcare data — I understand what it means in a clinical context and what decisions it should drive.

I've built three portfolio projects that reflect this intersection: optimizing laboratory turnaround time, segmenting medical sales customers by value, and measuring retail promotion effectiveness. Each one was built around a real question — not just to practice tools.

"Most data analysts learn the tools first and the domain later. I have both — and that's a rare combination for healthcare analytics roles."
